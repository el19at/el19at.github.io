Task Scheduling Simulator

Developed a simulator to solve task scheduling challenges in heterogeneous parallel systems, inspired by real-world constraints from autonomous vehicle platforms like Mobileye. The system compares heuristic scheduling strategies on real DAG-based workloads (~5000 tasks per file), achieving improved efficiency over greedy baselines using a novel median-based method and practical online scheduling techniques.
